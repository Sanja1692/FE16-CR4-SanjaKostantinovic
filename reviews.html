Miles Davis

I was immediately impressed by the size of the performing space. It was much larger than I imagined it when viewed from the outside. I sat in the middle of the first balcony, which afforded me an excellent view of the orchestra.

The Poly PAC is a fantastic place to experience a concert. All of the subtleties of the music were heard very easily due to the excellent acoustics, and from my relatively inexpensive seat, it was easy to see all of the musicians of the orchestra. I will look forward to future performances by this fine local orchestra in this excellent concert hall.
